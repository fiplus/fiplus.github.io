Welcome to the Fi+ Android App!

Are You Having Problems...

  • Finding enough people for your pickup basketball game?
  • Or maybe, finding fitness events you want to participate in?

Fear Not!

  • With Fi+ you can create fitness events on the fly.
  • Easily connect with others who want to participate.
  • And find the events you want to join.

How Does it Work?

  • Fi+ offers the basic matchmaking and event creating functionalities of popular Apps such as MeetUp and Facebook.
  • Event based active community. No groups means low barriers, when joining events, and no inactive communities.
  • Events are open to all users of the App. Expanding your network of possible participants.
  • Creators can allow suggestions for time and location. Gauge your audience and take your pick.
    • With Fi+, you can save your time and energy for the game!
